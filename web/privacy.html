Privacy Policy - IronTracker

IronTracker is a mobile application for tracking your fitness journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent understanding of how our app collects and handles your data.

Camera Usage:

IronTracker uses your device's camera to capture images, specifically for the purpose of tracking fitness progress. However, please be assured that we do not collect or store any of the images or other personal data from your camera. All data and images captured are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any external locations. We use an AI model locally, and no data is used or will ever be used to train AI models.

Data Storage:

All data generated by IronTracker, including images, workouts, and any other related information, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor do we collect, share, or transmit any of it to external servers or third parties.

Data Security:

We take the security and privacy of your data seriously. It is your responsibility to safeguard your device and the data stored within it.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about our privacy policy or the IronTracker app, please feel free to contact us at irontracker3@gmail.com.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. We encourage you to periodically review this policy for any updates or modifications.
